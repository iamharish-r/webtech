my Tunes

Downloads in seconds, entertains forever.


Discover myTunes, the home of endless entertainment. Music, movies, TV shows, and more come together here. It’s also where you can join StreamSquid Music and stream — or download and play offline — over 50 million songs, ad-free.

Music

50 million songs. Zero ads.



Stream over 50 million songs, ad-free. Or download albums and tracks to listen to offline. All the music in your personal myTunes library — no matter where it came from — lives right alongside the Pears Music catalog. Start your free three-month trial with no commitment, and cancel anytime.

Pears Music is available in myTunes, and for iOS and Android devices.
